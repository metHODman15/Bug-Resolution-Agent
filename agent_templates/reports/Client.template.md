## How to Verify the Fix

After making the recommended changes:

1. Open one of the affected records
2. Verify that the fields show the correct values
3. Test the functionality that was previously failing
4. Confirm that the issue is resolved

If the issue persists after following these steps, please escalate to your Salesforce administrator.

## When to Escalate

Escalate to your Salesforce administrator if:

- The recommended changes do not resolve the issue
- You do not have permission to make the necessary changes
- The issue affects more records than originally identified

When escalating, provide this report and the example record URLs.
